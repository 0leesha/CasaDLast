Our Story

From a vision to transform home shopping to becoming your trusted partner in creating beautiful living spaces.

The Beginning

CASA D LAST was born from a simple observation: finding the perfect furniture and home decor shouldn't be a challenge. Our founders, students of Shri Kiritbhai D. Patel Umiya BCA College, noticed how difficult it was for people to shop for home interiors.

Customers had to visit multiple physical stores, struggle with limited variety, and spend countless hours searching for the right pieces. There was no centralized platform that made home interior shopping convenient, inspiring, and accessible.

Our Journey

2024

The Idea

During our final year at Umiya BCA College, we identified the gap in the online home furnishing market. The idea for CASA D LAST was conceptualized as our System Development Project.

2025

Development Phase

Under the guidance of Prof. Jalpa Ribadiya, we began building the platform using modern technologies - Python, Django, and MySQL, creating a robust and scalable solution.

2026

Launch & Growth

CASA D LAST officially launched, bringing together furniture sellers and home decor enthusiasts on a single platform, making home interior shopping simple and enjoyable.

"We believe every home tells a story, and we're here to help you write yours with furniture and decor that reflects your unique style and personality."

- The CASA D LAST Team

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize the home furnishing experience by providing a centralized, user-friendly online platform that connects customers with quality furniture and home decor. We aim to save time, offer variety, and inspire design creativity for every home.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and preferred destination for home interiors, where every customer finds inspiration, quality, and convenience. We envision a future where beautiful homes are accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or location.
