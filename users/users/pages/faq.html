Orders & Payment

Browse products, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, enter shipping details, select payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately.

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), net banking, UPI, digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay), and EMI options on select cards. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's dispatched. Log into your account, go to "My Orders," and click "Cancel Order." Full refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and secure payment gateways. We never store your complete card details on our servers.

Yes, we offer EMI options on orders above ₹5,000 with select banks. You can choose 3, 6, 9, or 12-month EMI plans at checkout. Interest rates vary by bank.
Shipping & Delivery

Delivery time varies by location: Metro cities (3-5 days), Tier 2 cities (5-7 days), Other locations (7-10 days). Exact timeline is shown at checkout based on your pin code.

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. Use this on our "Track Order" page or your account dashboard to see real-time delivery status.

Shipping is free for orders above ₹15,000 (metro cities), ₹20,000 (tier 2 cities), and ₹25,000 (other locations). Below these amounts, charges apply based on your zone.

We deliver to most serviceable pin codes across India. Enter your pin code at checkout to check availability. Remote areas may have extended delivery times.
Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return policy from delivery date. Items must be unused, in original packaging with tags attached. Some products like custom-made furniture are not eligible for return.

Log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the item, and click "Return." Choose a reason, schedule pickup, and our team will collect the item. Returns are free for defective/damaged products.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Yes, exchanges are available for size, color, or design variations within 7 days. Initiate an exchange request from your account. We'll arrange pickup and delivery of the replacement item.
